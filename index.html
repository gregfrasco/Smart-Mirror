Weather

The Weather Module includes the current temperature, forecast icon, forecast status, the weeks' status, the participation percentage, and if it's ok to bike today.

Time and Transit

The Time and Transit Module includes the time and date and the local transit time. The transit times comes from the Massachusetts Bay Transit Authority (MBTA) RESTful API to get the two closest stations and there trains arrival times.
